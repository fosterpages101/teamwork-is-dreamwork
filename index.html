1. Direct Communication

When you suspect a data issue, please directly address our data engineer (that's me!). A quick, "Hey [Your Name], I'm seeing a potential data issue with [specific data point/table] impacting [feature X]" is a game-changer!

2. Quick Prioritization

Scenario: The "Superpower" Way!

Result: Fast resolution, clear ownership, and a happy, productive team!
